CSV Help

CSV files can be edited with numerous programs such as Excel, other sheet editors, or even just a regular text editor.

The slides CSV is broken up into 4 columns of data. These columns are the date, the day name, the slide background color, and the slide text color.

An example row for an A Day is 2018-05-21,A (the comma indicates the separation of columns in spreadsheet editors like Excel). All dates are written in the YYYY-MM-DD format, and A or B days only need to be written as a single capital letter (not "A Day") with no colors specified (since they are automatically selected).

An example row for a custom day could look like 2018-06-19,Regents Testing,pink,black. This would make a slide that says "Regents Testing" with black text on a pink background.

Please be sure every week is a group of 5 rows (for Mon-Fri), write "No School" for the day name if there's no school on a weekday. The first row must start on a Monday.

Once you finish editing your slides .csv file, simply select it in Calendar Upload section, and the process will begin automatically. It can take some time and the page may freeze depending on how powerful the computer you are on is. All the image processing is done right here in the browser.

The progress bar on the bottom will track the status on the slide generation, and then when it is complete a .zip file will be automatically downloaded containing all the slides.
